What We Offer

Design descriptions are a way to communicate the design of a product or system to other people. They can be used when creating an electronic or paper document or when speaking with someone about the design. A design description contains information about the purpose of the design, how it should be used, and how it looks. The goal is to make sure that everyone who uses or sees the design understands what it is and why it exists.
